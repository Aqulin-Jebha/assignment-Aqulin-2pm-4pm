How to make a Tea

  1. Add 1 cup of water to a saucepan and let it boil.
  2. Add 1 to 2 teaspoons of tea powder to the boiling water.
  3. Let it boil for 30 to 40 seconds so the flavor comes out, Mix well.
  4. Add 2 teaspoons of sugar
  5. Let the tea boil on medium flame for 2 to 3 minutes until it gets a good color and aroma.For stronger tea, boil for a bit longer.
  6. Use a tea strainer to filter the tea into a cup.
