Projects

Iot Based Assistance System For Alzheimer's Patients

Developed an IoT-based assistance system to support Alzheimer’s patients in daily activities. Integrated face recognition using machine learning to identify caregivers and known individuals. Designed a wearable device to track patient movement and ensure safety. It focused on improving patient independence and reducing caregiver workload through smart automation.

River Cleaning Boat

Designed and built a semi-autonomous river cleaning boat to remove floating waste from water bodies. Implemented a conveyor-belt mechanism to collect plastic, debris, and other pollutants efficiently. Equipped the boat with DC motors for controlled movements.
