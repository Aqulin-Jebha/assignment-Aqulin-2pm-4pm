Anjurully Tunel!

Located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, Anjurully Tunnel is a marvel of engineering and a gateway to the picturesque hill station of Munnar. This tunnel, carved through the mountains, offers travelers a unique experience as they journey through lush greenery and mist-covered landscapes. The tunnel is not just a passage but a scenic route that enhances the beauty of the surrounding tea plantations and rolling hills. A visit to Anjurully Tunnel is a must for anyone exploring Munnar, providing both a thrilling drive and stunning views that capture the essence of Kerala's natural beauty.

Vagamon

Vagamon, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, is a serene hill station known for its lush greenery, rolling meadows, and cool climate. This tranquil destination offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, making it ideal for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Vagamon is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, including tea gardens, pine forests, and mist-covered hills that create a magical ambiance. Visitors can indulge in activities such as trekking, paragliding, and exploring the nearby waterfalls and caves. With its peaceful environment and breathtaking views, Vagamon is a hidden gem that promises a rejuvenating experience for all who visit.
