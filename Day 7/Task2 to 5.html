Yamaha RX 100

Overview

The Yamaha RX 100 is a classic motorcycle known for its performance and reliability. It was first introduced in the 1980s and quickly became popular among motorcycle enthusiasts.

Specifications

  • Engine: 98cc, 2-stroke, single-cylinder
  • Power: 11.2 HP @ 7500 RPM
  • Torque: 10.5 Nm @ 6500 RPM
  • Transmission: 4-speed manual
  • Top Speed: Approximately 110 km/h
  • Fuel Capacity: 10 liters

Features

The Yamaha RX 100 features a lightweight design, making it easy to handle and maneuver. It also has a responsive throttle and a comfortable seating position, making it suitable for both city commuting and longer rides.
