Munnar

Munnar is a beautiful hill station located in the Western Ghats mountain range in Kerala, India. It is known for its picturesque landscapes, tea plantations, and pleasant climate. Munnar is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors with its natural beauty, wildlife sanctuaries, and opportunities for trekking and exploring nature.

Attractions in Munnar

  • Tea Gardens: Munnar is famous for its sprawling tea plantations that offer stunning views and a chance to learn about tea production.
  • Eravikulam National Park: Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, this park offers trekking trails and panoramic views of the Western Ghats.
  • Mattupetty Dam: A popular spot for boating and picnics, surrounded by lush greenery and hills.
  • Top Station: Known for its breathtaking views of the valley and the distant plains of Tamil Nadu.
  • Attukal Waterfalls: A scenic waterfall located amidst the hills, perfect for nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Munnar is from September to March when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
