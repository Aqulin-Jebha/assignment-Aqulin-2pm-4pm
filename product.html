pen

description

A pen is a common writing instrument used to apply ink onto paper or other surfaces. It is designed to provide smooth, precise, and permanent writing. A typical pen consists of a barrel (the body you hold), an ink reservoir, and a tip or nib that controls the flow of ink.

Uses of Pen

For taking notes, writing essays, letters, or exams.

Features

Smooth Ink Flow: Writes evenly without skipping or smudging.

Comfort Grip: Designed for long writing sessions without hand fatigue.

Durable Design: Made with high-quality materials for long-lasting use
